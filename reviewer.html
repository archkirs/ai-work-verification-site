What an HCR includes

Human Contribution Record

Human contribution
What the person planned, selected, changed, and approved
AI assistance
Which tasks AI supported and how the output was used
Submitted materials
Which drafts, files, conversations, versions, or screenshots were provided
Evidence status
What is declared, supported, or not verified
Open questions
What still requires reviewer judgment or follow-up

The review problem

A final file shows too little. A long AI chat shows too much.

An HCR gives the reviewer a readable starting point.

The creator provides the materials, checks the generated record, and shares it with the reviewer.

What the reviewer receives

One short report with the main review questions.

How it works

A clearer review process.

1

Invite a creator

Ask for an HCR for a specific piece of work.

2

The creator uploads materials

The creator provides the final work and relevant process materials.

3

The MVP creates a draft

The creator checks and approves the structured HCR.

4

Review and follow up

Read the record and request more detail when questions remain.

Limits

The HCR supports review. It does not replace it.

Not an AI detectorNot legal certificationNot copyright or ownership proofDoes not replace reviewer judgment
