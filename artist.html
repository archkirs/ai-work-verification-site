What an HCR includes

Materials
Sketches, drafts, versions, notes, references, and source files
Open questions
What is supported and what remains uncertain

Why this helps

The finished work does not show the decisions behind it.

When AI was used, a client, editor, publisher, contest, or audience may misunderstand the human role.

An HCR shows the creator’s direction, selections, revisions, and final choices.

How it works

From creative project to a reviewable HCR.

1

2

Upload materials if selected

3

The MVP prepares a draft HCR

It covers the human role, AI assistance, submitted materials, and open questions.

4

Check and share

Approve the facts before sharing with a client, editor, publisher, contest, or platform.

Limits

The record explains the process. It does not certify ownership.
