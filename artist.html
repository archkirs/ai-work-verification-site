What you send / what you get

Show the process behind the final creative work.

The record helps reduce the feeling that AI did everything by showing your choices, direction, editing, and final responsibility.

You can include

  • final image, text, design, or comic page
  • AI chats or prompts
  • sketches, drafts, versions
  • notes, references, source files

The record shows

  • your idea and direction
  • your selections and edits
  • how AI helped
  • what the record cannot prove

Why this matters

A final image, text, design, or comic page does not show the creative process.

When AI was used, people may misunderstand the human role.

A Human Contribution Record gives a short, honest summary of the process.

What the record can show

Creative direction, AI help, and process materials.

The record is not a copyright claim. It is a short explanation of the creative process and the creator’s role.

Possible fields

Creative role what the person made, directed, or edited
Human decisions concept, selection, correction, edits, final approval
AI assistance where AI helped and how it was used
Limits what still needs reviewer judgment

What this is not

A process record, not a copyright certificate.

Not an AI detector Not legal certification Not copyright proof Not ownership proof Not a 100% human claim Not a rights clearance tool

FAQ

A record helps explain process. It does not replace review.

The pilot is based on materials the creator provides. It does not independently determine copyright status, ownership, publication rights, client acceptance, platform compliance, or originality.

It helps show human choices, direction, editing, selection, correction, and final responsibility.
