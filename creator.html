About

Creative work often has more process than a final file can show.

AI tools can help with research, concept exploration, writing, image generation, editing, layout, and revision. But a client or editor may still need to understand the creator’s role.

A Creator Process Dossier turns scattered process materials into a short reviewable summary: human direction, AI assistance, process evidence, source notes, and limitations.

FAQ

A dossier helps explain process. It does not replace review.

The pilot is based on submitted materials only. It does not independently determine copyright status, ownership, publication rights, client acceptance, platform compliance, or originality.

In the current pilot, records are created manually. Do not submit confidential materials until invited to a controlled pilot.
