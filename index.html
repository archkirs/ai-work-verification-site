FAQ

A record helps review. It does not replace review.

The pilot record is based on materials provided by the person who made the work. It does not decide originality, ownership, academic acceptance, platform compliance, or quality.

The goal is simple: make AI-assisted work more understandable by showing what the person did and how AI helped.
