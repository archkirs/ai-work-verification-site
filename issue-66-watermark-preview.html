Current working recommendation

Use C as the direction to refine first, not as an approved final layout. It gives stronger casual-reuse deterrence than one central mark while preserving more readability than a dense repeated field. The next design pass should test exact position, opacity, mark size, text density, light/dark adaptation and whether Comic and document Evidence require different placement rules within one shared watermark system.
